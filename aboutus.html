OUR ADVENTURES

Adventourus, non-conformist with a purpose and passion…this is what firefox and our tribe stands for. Our story began with and stands for the love of biking.

Our bikes hit the road in 2005 and since then Firefox has been the most loved bike brand in India among millennials. This unique pride of owning a Firefox within our customers, fuels our passion to innovate and bring in great quality products which are a joy to ride.

Firefox was launched in 2005, when cycling was still seen as a traditional and conventional medium of transportation. Being the pathbreakers, we set about revolutionising biking as a means of transformation. We are driven by the sole purpose of making great quality bikes and enrich customer experience. Every Firefox bike speaks for itself, a product of labour and passion they are configured to provide a great riding experience. Firefox has ignited a wave of revolution and continues to write new definitions of adventure everyday.

EVERYONE

ADVENTURE FOR

As part of its corporate social responsibility, Firefox Bikes has collaborated with Volunteer4India to conceptualize #EverydayAbility program.

It is an initiative to encourage, facilitate and demonstrate equal opportunity, inclusivity and potential employability of the Differently-abled. The unique program is being brought to life by our NGO Partner, Adventures Beyond Barriers Foundation. Through this movement, Firefox Bikes with Adventures Beyond Barriers Foundation and Volunteer4India, has helped people climb to new heights, trek through various terrains, cycle many miles up, and even experience scuba diving!
